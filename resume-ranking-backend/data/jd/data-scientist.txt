Data Scientist - Job Description
Company: DataTech Solutions
Location: Remote/Hybrid
Experience: 3+ years

Position Overview
We are seeking a skilled Data Scientist to analyze complex datasets, build predictive models, and deliver actionable insights that drive business decisions. You will work with cross-functional teams to solve challenging problems using advanced analytics and machine learning.

Key Responsibilities
Collect, clean, and analyze large structured and unstructured datasets

Develop machine learning models and predictive algorithms

Perform exploratory data analysis to identify trends and patterns

Create data visualizations and reports for stakeholders

Collaborate with engineering teams to deploy models in production

Design A/B testing frameworks and statistical experiments

Communicate complex findings to non-technical audiences

Required Skills
Programming: Python, R, SQL

Machine Learning: Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch

Statistics: Hypothesis testing, regression analysis, statistical modeling

Visualization: Tableau, Power BI, matplotlib, seaborn

Big Data: Spark, Hadoop (preferred)

Cloud: AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud experience

Qualifications
Master's/PhD in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field

3+ years of hands-on data science experience

Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking skills

Experience with data mining, feature engineering, and model validation

Excellent communication skills for presenting insights

What We Offer
Competitive salary ($95K-$140K)

Health benefits and flexible work arrangements

Professional development opportunities

Access to cutting-edge tools and technologies
